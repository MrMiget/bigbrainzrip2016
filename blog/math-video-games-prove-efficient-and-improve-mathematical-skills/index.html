Math Video Games Prove Efficient and Improve Mathematical Skills

Posted on May 28, 2015 by Wallaroo

Math can be one of the most frustrating subjects you can ever learn. At an early age, we go to school and learn how to add, subtract, divide, and multiply. As adults, it seems simple enough because the older we get, the easier it is for us to comprehend. Unfortunately, it’s not always so simple for kids. Each child goes at his or her own pace and has different ways of learning. Some children learn to add by counting on their fingers or by creating a song to help them with their times tables. Since each child has a different method of learning, it can be difficult to know the best way to teach basic addition and subtraction skills. Teaching them multiplication can be even harder.

Video games have been around for quite a while now and are very popular among youth. Video games often have a bad connotation as they seem to have some connection with kids harboring laziness and other undesirable traits. Despite the bad rap video games often receive, research is continually being done to see how they can be used in a more positive way, including for educational purposes.

 

 

Big Brainz has come up with a fun way for kids to learn multiplication. Big Brainz has created a math video game that has produced some amazing results, increasing students’ mathematical knowledge. The new game is called Timez Attack.

 

Timez Attack is an adventure game that takes the player through multiple worlds by solving multiplication problems. Big Brainz claims that this educational game not only helps kids learn multiplication facts but also helps them become fluent in math fundamentals.

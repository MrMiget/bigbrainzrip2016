Tips and Research

When help isn't helpful
When a child first begins playing Timez Attack, they will struggle.  They'll need to learn to walk, run, jump, etc.  They'll also struggle with the mental challenges the game throws at them.  In order to keep the child's interest, Timez Attack is designed to build on the skills that they're constantly learning, so if you help them early on by steering, jumping, or answering questions for them, they'll be unprepared for all the fun that lies ahead.

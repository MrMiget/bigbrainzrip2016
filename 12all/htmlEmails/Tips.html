I'm guessing you've had a chance to play with Timez Attack, and I thought I'd give you a few little tips that most people find helpful:

  • To skip to a level, just start a new game and give it the name of the level you want to jump to, like "3s", "4s", "9s", etc. 

  • To brighten your screen, press the "B" key.  

  • Make sure to boost the game's resolution up from its default settings (if your PC can handle it).  Click on "Options".

  • To see your FPS, press the "G" key.
